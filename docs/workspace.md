Overview
A Workspace is the primary environment in HiveForge where users organize their Digital Workforce, Missions, knowledge, and projects.
Think of a Workspace as a digital organization. It contains everything required for a specific business, team, client, or personal initiative.
Each Workspace is isolated, ensuring its Digital Workforce, knowledge, and history remain organized and context-aware.
________________________________________
Why Workspaces?
People don't have one life.
They have multiple contexts.
Examples:
🏢 My Startup

🏪 Retail Store

💼 Client Projects

🎓 University

🏠 Personal
Instead of mixing everything into one chat, HiveForge keeps every environment separate.

3. Knowledge Hub
Every Workspace has a dedicated knowledge base.
Supported knowledge:
•	PDFs 
•	Word documents 
•	Excel files 
•	Images 
•	Notes 
•	URLs 
•	Company policies 
•	Product catalogs 
•	Previous reports 
This knowledge provides context for Digital Workers during Mission execution.

Design Principles
Context Isolation
Every Workspace has independent memory and knowledge.
________________________________________
Scalability
Users can create unlimited Workspaces.
________________________________________
Flexibility
Each Workspace supports different Digital Workers depending on its purpose.
________________________________________
Persistence
All Missions, outputs, and knowledge remain available for future reference.

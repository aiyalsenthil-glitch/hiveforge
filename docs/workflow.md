Overview
HiveForge is designed around a simple principle:
Users define goals. HiveForge handles execution.
The user never manages AI prompts, models, or workflows directly. Instead, they focus on outcomes while Hive Core coordinates the Digital Workforce behind the scenes.

________________________________________
UX Principles
Outcome First
Users think in goals, not prompts.
Visible Intelligence
Users can observe planning, collaboration, and execution.
Progressive Disclosure
Simple by default, detailed when needed.
Transparency
Users always know:
•	Who is working 
•	What is happening 
•	What's completed 
•	What's next 
Minimal Learning Curve
Creating a Mission should feel as natural as sending a message.

Overview
The Digital Workforce is the core execution layer of HiveForge. It consists of specialized Digital Workers, each designed with a unique role, expertise, memory, tools, and responsibilities.
Unlike traditional AI assistants that attempt to answer every question, Digital Workers focus on their area of expertise while collaborating with other workers to accomplish a Mission.
Hive Core dynamically assembles the appropriate Digital Workforce for each Mission, ensuring that only the necessary workers participate.

Dynamic Workforce Assembly
HiveForge does not activate every worker for every Mission.
Instead, Hive Core selects workers based on:
•	Mission Type 
•	Required Skills 
•	Available Knowledge 
•	Task Dependencies 
This keeps execution efficient and relevant.

Core Design Principles
•	Specialization: Each worker focuses on a well-defined domain. 
•	Collaboration: Workers exchange information to produce coherent results. 
•	Modularity: New workers can be added without altering the core system. 
•	Transparency: Users can observe which workers are active and what they are producing. 
•	Scalability: The platform can evolve from four workers to hundreds of specialized Digital Workers over time.
